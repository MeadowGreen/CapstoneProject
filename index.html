Our Values

Honesty

At Nature Aspect, you will never meet a dishonest member. We work hard to create a trustworthy environment, suitable for you to feel comfortable with your animal in our care.

Caring and Compassion

This sanctuary is caring and compassionate not only to all animals, but to our customers. We care about your needs and will treat you like family.

Respect

We respect you, your pets, and our animals. We respect everyone who steps on foot of our sanctuary and will reciprocate the best customer service you've ever experienced.

Nature Aspect

About Us

Nature Aspect is a utopia for every and any animal. Whether orphaned, sick, injured, or simply donated to the sanctuary, our goal is to create a loving and safe environment for animals in need of a home. Animals ranging from wild bears to the smallest guinea pigs are welcomed. While acting as an animal sanctuary, we also provide supplies such as food, enrichment, and bedding for purchase as well as tours of our sanctuary.

We hope to inspire people to care about animals and the world around them.

Our Products and Services

Pet Boarding

Bring your pets to us while on vacation, they will never know they even left home.

Petting area

Goats, sheep, dear, llamas, and more animals are available for up close and personal experience. Visit our petting zoo to meet all of our soft and friendly animals!

Tours

Want to learn more about animals? Looking for a nice trip with the family? Schedule today to have an in-depth tour of our sanctuary.

Pet supplies

We carry a wide range of supplies for many animals, nothing that we don't use with our animals. All of our supplies are vet approved from our vet team.

Adoption

Looking for a new family dog? Cat or rabbit? Look no further! We take in and foster many pets until they are ready to go to their forever home.

Donation

We always accept donations, whether that be money donations or animal donations. If you can no longer take care of a pet, bring them here and we will take care of them until we can find them a forever home.
